Geovation Geospatial Innovation Awards 2024

We won!

Women+ in Geospatial has been honored with the Contribution to Diversity award at the 2024 Geovation Geospatial Innovation Awards. This recognition underscores our commitment to fostering diversity and inclusion within the geospatial community. Our co-founder and director, Dr. Julia Wagemann, alongside INSPIRE Pillar Lead, Mariam Crichton, proudly accepted the award, reflecting the collective achievements of our dedicated community. Being nominated among such distinguished initiatives, we are particularly proud of this achievement. This award validates our ongoing efforts to champion a more diverse and inclusive industry. We extend our gratitude to everyone who supports and collaborates with us in this vital mission. Thank you for being part of our journey towards greater diversity and equity in geospatial.
