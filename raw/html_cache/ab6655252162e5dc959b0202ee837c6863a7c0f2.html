We support women affected by the criminal justice system and campaign to end the harm it causes

Every year, thousands of women in England and Wales are swept into the criminal justice system, often due to poverty, homelessness, mental ill-health, and domestic abuse.

Image by Fanny Beckman

Approximately 100,000 women in England and Wales are swept up into the criminal justice system every year due to systemic failings in support and protection. We provide specialist support in prisons and communities, tailored to women’s needs to help them rebuild their lives. 

With women’s lived experience at the heart of our work, we campaign for radical change and community-based alternatives that address the root causes of offending, so fewer women are harmed by the criminal justice system.
