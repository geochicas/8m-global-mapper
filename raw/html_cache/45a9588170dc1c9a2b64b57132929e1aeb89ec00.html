📣 We take the streets again this year in a feminist strike for liberation.

📍Friday 8th of March 2024 6.30pm

📍Location Soho Square, London

 

From London to Palestine, Abya Yala to Kurdistan, our fight for liberation and solidarity must cross borders. None of us are free until all of us are free.

The freedom of Palestinians is intrinsic to our collective freedom against all forms of oppression. As feminists we support all liberation movements to combat injustice: we are building a movement to overcome colonial, imperialist and patriarchal systems, to build new more just worlds together.

We strike because around the world our siblings are losing their lives to patriarchal, imperialist and colonial powers.

We strike to grieve our siblings.

We strike because we believe another world is possible.

We strike for collective liberation.

 

Join us! Share this with all your friends! See you on the streets!

If your collective/organising group wants to join the call for the 8th of March, get in touch!
