HAPPENING AT UNIONSWA

International Women’s Day is just around the corner. Join us in commemorating International Women’s Day and celebrate the remarkable contributions of women, and hear from inspiring unionists.
FIND OUT MORE International Women’s Day Rally 2026
To honour the achievements of Union women and advance our fight for gender equality, UnionsWA is proud to host our International Women’s Day Conference 2026. Our theme for the Conference, Gender Equality: It’s for Every Body, reflects our commitment to justice and fairness for all women and every worker and aligns with the UN Women Australia’s official IWD 2026 theme, Balance the Scales, which calls for fair, inclusive, and accessible justice for every woman and girl.
FIND OUT MORE 2026 International Women’s Day Conference
The annual State Wage Case sets the Minimum Wage and Award Wage Rates for workers in the WA industrial relations system. Last year, Unions secured a much needed 3.75% wage increase. We’re fighting to secure another round of increases for low paid workers. But we need your help.
