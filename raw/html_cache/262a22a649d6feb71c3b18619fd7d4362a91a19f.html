Welcome to Girls In Technology Liberia (GITL), Non-Profit Organization

Empowering Women & Transforming Lives

At GITL, our mission is to empower women and girls through technology. Together, we can create opportunities, bridge the digital gap, and inspire change. Join us in making a difference. Every effort counts!

Our Services

We empower women & girls across Liberia.

We provide digital skills training, mentorship, and opportunities for personal and professional growth. Our mission is to bridge the gender gap in technology and create a future where women and girls are leaders in innovation and change.
